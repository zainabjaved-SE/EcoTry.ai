# 👗 ECOTRY — AI-Powered Virtual Try-On WebApp

> **Try before you buy — from the comfort of your home.**
## 🌟 About ECOTRY

**ECOTRY** is an AI-powered Virtual Try-On Web Application developed as a Final Year Project at **Govt. College University Faisalabad (2026)**.

The idea was born from a real problem faced by online shoppers in Pakistan:

> *"Will this color suit me? Will it fit me? How will I look in this outfit?"*

ECOTRY answers all of these questions — **instantly and intelligently** — before you even place an order.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 👗 1. Virtual Try-On
- Try on outfits in **real-time using your live camera**
- Or simply **upload your photo** and see the look instantly
- No physical fitting room needed!

### 📐 2. Smart Size Recommendation
- AI-powered size suggestion based on body measurements
- Reduces wrong purchases and returns
- Helps customers find their **perfect fit every time**

### 🎨 3. Skin Tone & Style Advisor
- Detects your **skin tone automatically**
- Recommends **colors and outfit styles** that best suit your complexion
- Personalized fashion advice — like having your own stylist!

## 🚀 How It Works

```
User opens ECOTRY
        ↓
Choose: Live Camera  OR  Upload Photo
        ↓
Select outfit / clothing item
        ↓
ECOTRY AI overlays outfit on your image
        ↓
Size Recommendation Engine suggests best fit
        ↓
Skin Tone Advisor recommends best colors & styles
        ↓
Shop with full confidence! ✅
```
